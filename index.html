About EI

The Centre for Environmental Intelligence (CEI) is an interdisciplinary research cluster focused on the responsible use of AI and data science to solve problems around environment and sustainability. Environmental Intelligence is a guiding concept that intersects environment, AI & data science, and responsible innovation. The CEI combines research projects, a doctoral programme, postgraduate education and community-building activities. A big part of our research approach is engagement with external partners who help us ensure our research has impact.

  • Biotechnology
  • Clean Energy
