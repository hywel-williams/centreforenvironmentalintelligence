About EI

Environmental Intelligence is an interdisciplinary research concept focused on the responsible use of AI and data science to solve problems around environment and sustainability. The Centre for Environmental Intelligence is a research community at University of Exeter that brings together academics, students and practitioners around the study of EI, combining research projects, a doctoral programme, postgraduate education and community-building activities. A big part of the CEI research approach is engagement with external partners, who help ensure our research has impact.
